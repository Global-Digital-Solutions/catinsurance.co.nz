About CatInsurance.co.nz

NZ's independent cat insurance comparison site — helping Kiwi cat owners find the right cover since 2024.

Our Mission

CatInsurance.co.nz exists to help New Zealand cat owners make confident, informed decisions about pet insurance. We independently compare all major NZ cat insurance providers — analysing premiums, coverage, excess structures, co-payments, and claim satisfaction — so you don't have to.

How We Compare Providers

We research all publicly available policy documents
We track customer satisfaction and complaint data
We update our comparisons at least twice per year
We are not affiliated with or funded by any insurer
We disclose affiliate commissions transparently
We use NZ spelling and NZ-specific pricing data

How This Site Works

CatInsurance.co.nz is operated by Cover4You and is an independent comparison website. When you click through to an insurer's website and purchase a policy, we may receive a commission. This does not affect the independence of our comparisons or the price you pay.

All information on this site is for general informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. For personalised advice, contact a Financial Adviser authorised by the Financial Markets Authority (FMA). You can find registered advisers at fma.govt.nz.

NZ Insurance Regulation

All cat insurance providers listed on this site are regulated by the Financial Markets Authority (FMA) and operate under the Insurance (Prudential Supervision) Act 2010. New Zealand's insurance industry body is the Insurance Council of New Zealand (ICNZ).

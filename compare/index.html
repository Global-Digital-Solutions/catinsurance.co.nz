Our Independent Assessment of Cat Insurance in 2026

New Zealand currently has five specialist cat insurance providers — Petcover, Southern Cross Pet Insurance, PD Insurance, SPCA Pet Insurance, and Cove — plus broader pet insurers like AA and Tower that include cat cover. This comparison focuses on the five dedicated providers, which consistently offer more competitive pricing and deeper coverage for cats.

The most important things to compare are not just monthly premiums. Annual benefit limits, co-payment percentages, excess options, and waiting periods all affect how much you actually receive back when you claim.

Our analysis found significant variation: on a $5,000 cancer treatment claim, a policyholder with PD Insurance (no co-payment, $150 excess) would receive $4,850 back, while the same claim with Southern Cross (20% co-payment, $250 excess) yields $3,800 — a $1,050 difference annually.

All information is based on publicly available policy documents and pricing as of April 2026. Premiums are indicative and depend on your cat's age, breed, and chosen excess. Always read the full policy wording before purchasing.
